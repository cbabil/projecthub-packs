# Security Policy

## Reporting a vulnerability
- Email `security@projecthub.dev` with a detailed description and reproduction steps.
- Please do not open public issues for security reports.

## Response commitments
- Acknowledge receipt within 2 business days.
- Provide an initial assessment within 5 business days.
- Coordinate on a fix timeline and disclosure plan with the reporter.

## Scope
- Templates and libraries contained in this repository.
- CI workflows and configuration that impact repository integrity.

## Preferred languages
- English is preferred; concise reports help us respond faster.
