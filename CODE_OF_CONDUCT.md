# Code of Conduct

We follow the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/) as our community standard. By participating, you agree to uphold these principles.

## Reporting
Report unacceptable behavior to the maintainers at `conduct@projecthub.dev`. Reports are handled confidentially and responses will include next steps within five business days.

## Enforcement
- Repeated or severe violations may lead to temporary or permanent community exclusion.
- Maintainers may remove content or block contributors to protect the community.
